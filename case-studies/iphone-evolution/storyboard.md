# iPhone Evolution Storyboard

Style: Apple keynote meets museum exhibit. No logos, no product photography.

## Arc

The phone becomes a software canvas, then an ecosystem, then an identity layer, then an AI surface.

## Scenes

1. 2007 Original iPhone: a black glass slab lights with a single touch ripple.
2. 2008 App Store: an empty screen grows an icon grid.
3. 2010 Retina: a chunky pixel grid tightens into smooth glass.
4. 2013 Touch ID: fingerprint ring becomes a security portal.
5. 2017 iPhone X: the home button fades as the screen expands.
6. 2023 USB-C: one clean cable line connects creative tools.
7. 2024 Apple Intelligence: a soft neural halo appears behind the device.

## Acceptance

- All labels are editable code text.
- Visuals remain abstract and non-branded.
- Each milestone has a source in `sources.md`.
