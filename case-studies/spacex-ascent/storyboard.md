# SpaceX Ascent Storyboard

Style: mission control documentary. No logos, no official photography.

## Arc

The story moves from reaching orbit to making launch systems reusable and scalable.

## Scenes

1. 2008 Falcon 1 Orbit: a thin ascent line breaks above the atmosphere.
2. 2010 Dragon Returns: a capsule arc returns through a telemetry corridor.
3. 2015 First Stage Landing: the booster line descends and locks to a pad.
4. 2020 Crew Dragon: two crew dots cross a mission board.
5. 2024 Starship Catch: tower arms catch a returning silhouette.
6. 2026 System Ambition: many arcs become a reusable network.

## Acceptance

- Motion uses orbital arcs, telemetry, and mission cards.
- All factual claims have sources.
- The tone is documentary, not fan poster.
